# Console Sign Up & Login

{% hint style="info" %}
This guide walks you through signing up and logging in to the bkend console.
{% endhint %}

## Overview

The bkend console supports social login (Google, GitHub) and magic link authentication. You can sign up and log in without setting a password.

***

## Supported Login Methods

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Google** | Sign in with your Google account |
| **GitHub** | Sign in with your GitHub account |
| **Magic Link** | Receive a one-time login link via email |

***

## Signing Up

1. Go to [https://console.bkend.ai](https://console.bkend.ai).
2. On the **Sign Up** page, choose a login method.

{% tabs %}
{% tab title="Google" %}
1. Click the **Continue with Google** button.
2. Select your Google account and approve the permissions.
3. You will be automatically redirected to the console home screen.
{% endtab %}
{% tab title="GitHub" %}
1. Click the **Continue with GitHub** button.
2. Sign in with your GitHub account and approve the permissions.
3. You will be automatically redirected to the console home screen.
{% endtab %}
{% tab title="Magic Link" %}
1. Enter your email address.
2. Click the **Send Magic Link** button.
3. Open the email and click the login link.
4. You will be automatically redirected to the console home screen.
{% endtab %}
{% endtabs %}

{% hint style="success" %}
Your account is automatically created on your first login. There is no separate sign-up process.
{% endhint %}

***

## Logging In

1. Go to [https://console.bkend.ai](https://console.bkend.ai).
2. Log in using the same method you used when signing up.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Magic links are only valid for a limited time after being sent. If expired, request a new one.
{% endhint %}

***

## Accepting an Invitation

If a team member sent you an invitation from the console:

1. Click the **Accept** link in the invitation email.
2. Choose a login method to authenticate.
3. You will automatically join the Organization.

***

## Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Cause | Solution |
|---------|-------|----------|
| Google login fails | Popup blocked | Allow popups in your browser settings |
| Magic link email not received | Spam filter | Check your spam folder, or resend |
| Blank screen after login | Cache issue | Clear your browser cache and try again |
